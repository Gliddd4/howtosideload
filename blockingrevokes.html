How to create your own DNS

Creating a NextDNS Account

First, you'll need to create an account with NextDNS:

  1. Open the NextDNS website
  2. Click the Sign Up button
  3. Enter your email & password

Recommended: Depending on your DNS usage, you may need to create a new account if you exceed 300k queries per month.

  1. Go to Temp-Mail
  2. Copy your temporary email after it loads
  3. Use this email to sign up for NextDNS

Set Up Your Deny List

Add these URLs to the Deny List tab to block Apple's servers from revoking you:

  • ocsp.apple.com
  • ocsp2.apple.com
  • valid.apple.com
  • crl.apple.com
  • certs.apple.com
  • appattest.apple.com
  • vpp.itunes.apple.com

Set Up Your Allow List

Add these URLs to the Allow List tab to enable services like Feather and ChatGPT:

  • register.appattest.apple.com
  • app.localhost.direct
